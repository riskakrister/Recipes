Shkmeruli Recipe

Shkmeruli

Ingredients

  • 350 - 500 g Chicken Fillet
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 3 tablespoons clarified butter or ghee
  • 1/4 cups of wheat flour
  • 1/4 cups of rice flour(Can be substituted with wheat flour)
  • 5 large garlic cloves(minced)
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of Milk
  • 1 teaspoon of Khmeli Suneli spice blend(Rimi sells it)
  • tablespoon of freshly ground black pepper

Cut the chicken fillets into smaller pieces(Whatever size you like them to be). Pat dry the chicken pieces and generously season with salt.

Melt 2 tablespoons of clarified butter in a medium-large heavy-bottomed pan or deep skillet. Heat the clarified butter until shimmering

Mix the rice- and wheat flour together in a bowl. Dredge the chicken pieces in flour and put those pieces into the frying pan of butter. Fry all of the chicken from the both sides until it is golden brown (about 5 minutes each side).

After all the pieces are fried, wipe the pan clean and melt the remaining 1 tablespoon of clarified butter. Add minced garlic to the pan and stirr it for about 20 seconds. Now pour in the milk and start simmering it, mix in the Khmeli Suneli spice mix if you have it. Lower the heat to medium-low and add the fried chicken pieces. Simmer it for 25 minutes until the chicken is tender and cokked through. If needed, season it with more salt and pepper.
