Vegan Hummus and Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • Toast of your choice
  • Hummus of your choice
  • Avocado
  • Tomato

Toast the bread and spread Hummus on the toast. Now slice the avocado into thin slices and put it on top of the toast. Next slice the tomato or chop it into pieces. However you like your tomato. Season the toast with salt and pepper and you are Ready to Go!
