Vegan Hummus and Avocado Toast

Description

Easy to make sandwich without meat so you wouldn't feel so heavy after eating. Perfect way to start your morning!

Ingredients

  • Toast of your choice
  • Hummus of your choice
  • Avocado
  • Tomato

Directions

  1. Toast the bread and spread Hummus on the toast.
  2. Now slice the avocado into thin slices and put it on top of the toast.
  3. Next slice the tomato or chop it into pieces. However you like your tomato.
  4. Season the toast with salt and pepper and you are Ready to Go!
