Vegan Wok
Description
Wok that's vegan won't take long time to make, is easier on your stomache and tastes really good with peanuts
Ingredients
  • Egg Noodles from Santa Maria: 1 pack
  • 2 Paprikas
  • 1 Broccoli flower head
  • 300g of Cashew nuts
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 pack of Sesame seeds
  • 1 whole Garlic
  • 1 whole Onion
  • Around 400g of Tofu
  • 200g of Butter
  • 200g of grated Cheese
  • Sauce of your choice, Recommended would be Santa Maria sauces
Directions
  1. Take the carrot and grate it. After that chop up Garlic, Onion, Paprikas and Broccoli. Slice Tofu into smaller pieces and put it aside

  2. Next you should boil Broccoli until it gets softer. Then take it out of the water.

  3. Take a Pan and put oil on it. First you should fry Garlic until it starts getting yellow/brown. Now add onions on top the Garlic. Fry the onion around 5 minutes and then add Paprika on top of it. Now cook it around 7-10 minutes and then add Broccoli and grated Carrot

  4. Now until the vegetables are cooking for about 10 minutes more you should start boiling egg noodles. When the Noodles are done add 200g of butter do it and mix it until it melts. Then add grated cheese to it.

  5. After that you should fry Tofu separetely and if the Tofu is ready you should add your choice of the Sauce to the Vegetable pan with Cashew nuts. If the mix is ready get your plate and start building your Wok on it.

  6. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top of the mix and you are ready to go :)
